# Glossary

## x402

The payment negotiation layer that tells a client a route is paid and how to satisfy it.

## Stream

A reusable payment authorization / settlement mechanism that avoids repeated one-off onchain payments.

## Verified Rental Twin

An NFT-backed digital twin of a productive rental asset. It is not, by itself, a direct legal deed transfer instrument.

## Evidence Root

A hash anchoring the private evidence bundle stored by the backend.

## Attestation

A signed claim from a role such as lawyer, inspector, valuer, insurer, registrar, issuer, or compliance operator.

## Asset Policy

The onchain freeze / dispute / revoke state for an RWA.
